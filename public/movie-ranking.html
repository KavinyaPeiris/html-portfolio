The Best Movies According to Kavinya

Top 3 movies of all time

1. The Art of Racing in the Rain

Heartfelt movie about a dog named Enzo who believes he will be reincarnated as a human. The story is narrated from Enzo's perspective, providing a unique and touching view of life, love, and the human condition. The film beautifully captures the bond between Enzo and his owner, Denny

2. Haseen Dilruba

A thrilling mystery drama that keeps you on the edge of your seat. The plot revolves around a woman accused of murdering her husband, with twists and turns that will leave you guessing until the very end. The performances are compelling, and the cinematography adds to the suspenseful atmosphere.

3. Me Before You

A romantic drama that explores the transformative power of love. The story follows Louisa Clark, a quirky young woman, who becomes a caregiver for Will Trevor, a wealthy man left paralyzed after an accident. Their relationship challenges both of them to see life from a different perspective, leading to profound personal growth and emotional depth.
